About us

In today's world, where technology is playing an increasingly important role, search and interaction between drivers and passengers are becoming easier and more convenient thanks to our innovative application. Our mission is to ensure comfortable and safe movement of people around the city, country or the world.

APPrideUSA offer a simple and convenient solution that allows drivers and passengers to find each other in a matter of minutes. Thanks to our platform, you will be able to reach your destination quickly and safely without worrying about finding the right transport.

Our app unites drivers and passengers based on their preferences and needs, which allows you to create individual routes and save time and money. We strive to ensure that every user can find a suitable option for their trip, whether it's a short trip around the city or a long trip to another city.

How does our app work? It's very simple: you specify your route, choose the departure time. Then our app selects the most suitable travel options for you from different drivers. You can choose the most suitable option for you and contact the driver to clarify all the details of the trip.

One of the main advantages of our application is security. All drivers have reviews from previous passengers.

Our team APPrideUSA is constantly working to improve our application to make it even more convenient and secure for users.

Do not forget that sharing transport is not only convenient, but also profitable.
